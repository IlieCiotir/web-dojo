Looking around you see that the entire room is ravaged. Some of the ceiling beams have fallen and are now blocking the door. The door itself is a mess and through it you can see the burning hallway.

On the other side a mentor is looking at you.

-You survived, the dojo is under attack. You will have to get yourself out, the door is badly damaged, you'll have to fix it. I must go help the others. If you have quetions ask them out loud and I will respond. Oh and you better make yourself a weapon, sharpen one of the beams and make a spear.

The mentor leaves in the direction of the canteen.

You start moving the fallen beams out of the way and repair the broken door.
