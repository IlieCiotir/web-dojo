Realm 101

Hosted in the memory of an ancient quantum computer Ream 101 is a land of mythical beasts and hidden knowledge.
From the loud Code monkeys of Scratch to the Photoshop Mages and the Hermits of Java all the creatures living in it are using powerfull skills to shape and create the world around them.
Out of all the inhabitans of Realm 101 though, none is more powerfull than the wise Web Samurai.
Riding horses of pure HTML breed, wearing enchanted CSS battle armor and wielding mighty Javascript swords the Web Samurai are 101's builders and peacekeepers.
Using their arcane knowledge and the works of the other inhabitants they create the Websites of 101, magical portals used by everyone to travel in this world.
But such power does not come without sacrifices, for one to become a Web Samuray he/she must leave their family and join a Coder Dojo, where the ancient knowledge is taught by all-knowing Mentors.
And that is exactly what you planned to do after visiting the Oracle of Pseudorandom. It told you that you have been chosen to be one of the great Web Samurai heroes of Realm 101, and ever since you have spent all your free time studying what you could find about the Web Samurai in the library of the Code Academy.
