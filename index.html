About

I started this project while mentoring the HTML group at Coder Dojo Timisoara. It's meant to provide kids a fun and engaging way of practicing their development skills after they finish the courses available on Codecademy. The idea behind it is to provide an engaging story/adventure game to kids through which they can advance by coding their way out of situations.

In order to keep focus on learning I tried to keep the setup to a minimum. For the WebSamurai story no setup is required at all, in order to complete a text editor and a browser is sufficient.

If this sounds cool to you and you'd like to help out please contact me or just fork/branch the project on GitHub. You can also help as a non-developer by reviewing the text, sharing new story ideas or help improve this website.
